The Industry Bottleneck

Conventional batteries offer high energy but charge slowly and degrade quickly. Supercapacitors offer speed but lack capacity. This trade-off limits the potential of the electric revolution.

The Divitra Solution

Our fabric-based asymmetric architecture delivers Battery-level Energy with Capacitor-level Power. Inherently safe, ultra-fast charging, and built for a decade of use.
