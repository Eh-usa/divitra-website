Meet Divitra: Where Energy Meets Power!

Core Technology Basis

Divitra has developed a fabric-based, aqueous asymmetric supercapacitor that uniquely combines battery-level energy density (~300 Wh/kg) with capacitor-level power (~3 kW/kg), ultra-fast charging, and >5,000-cycle lifetime—overcoming the traditional energy–power trade-off.

Strategic Positioning: Replacing consumable batteries with permanent, high-power energy components.

1. Professional & Industrial High-Power Tools

Primary near-term commercial application where productivity is tied to uptime.

  • Instant Torque: >3 kW/kg power delivers peak performance with no voltage sag.
  • Zero Downtime: <5 minute ultra-fast charging during work breaks.
  • Durability: 5,000+ cycles mean the battery outlasts the tool itself.
  • Safety: Aqueous chemistry eliminates fire risk in harsh industrial sites.

2. Electric Micromobility & High-Utilization Fleets

Eliminating battery degradation and charging bottlenecks in urban transport.

  • Range + Speed: 300 Wh/kg for urban range with 5-10 min "opportunity charging."
  • Fleet Economics: Near-continuous operation with zero degradation anxiety.
  • Hybrid Entry: Can act as a "booster module" to extend Li-ion life 3×.
  • Target: E-rickshaws, e-scooter fleets, and last-mile delivery.

3. Grid-Edge Power Buffering & Renewables

High-impact response for modern grids and fluctuating renewable energy.

  • Millisecond Response: Absorbs and releases sudden power spikes instantly.
  • Infinite Cycling: Millions of micro-cycles supported over decades.
  • Low OpEx: No fire suppression systems needed; simpler permitting.
  • Grid Support: Ideal for solar smoothing and weak distribution grids.

4. Critical Backup & Medical Power Systems

Reliability-driven, premium markets with zero tolerance for interruption.

  • No Switch-over Lag: Instantaneous power delivery for UPS systems.
  • Wide Temperature Range: Reliable performance in non-AC environments.
  • Fit-and-Forget: 15-20 year service life with zero maintenance.
  • Safe for Indoor Use: Intrinsic safety for hospitals and data centers.

5. Fast-Charge Consumer & Public Infrastructure

Public-facing visibility demonstrating ultra-fast charging at scale.

  • Multi-User Load: Maintains fast charging speeds even under heavy simultaneous load.
  • Public Safety: Non-flammable aqueous cells for high-traffic public areas.
  • Scalable Form Factor: Roll-to-roll manufacturing lowers infrastructure CAPEX.
