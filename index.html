The Energy Compromise — Solved

The Problem

Batteries store energy but charge slowly and degrade. Supercapacitors charge fast but store very little energy. Safety, lifetime, and performance remain unresolved trade-offs.

The Divitra Advantage

Our fabric-based hybrid architecture delivers battery-level energy with capacitor-level power, enabling ultra-fast charging, long life, and inherently safe operation.

Divitra bridges batteries and supercapacitors using a fabric-based aqueous architecture.

Engineered at the Interface

Patented Hybrid Chemistry

Rare-earth enhanced asymmetric electrode design enables simultaneous high energy density and high power density in a single device.

Fabric-Based Platform

Electrodes are integrated into textile substrates using low-temperature, scalable manufacturing compatible with roll-to-roll processes.

Safe by Design

Aqueous, non-flammable electrolyte chemistry eliminates thermal runaway risks common in conventional lithium-ion systems.
