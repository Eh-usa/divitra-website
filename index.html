Fast-Charging Hybrid Energy Storage

Our Mission

Divitra is building a new class of hybrid energy storage that bridges the gap between batteries and supercapacitors—delivering battery-level energy with supercapacitor-level power, safety, and lifetime.

Technology

Our patent-pending, fabric-based asymmetric supercapacitor architecture enables ultra-fast charging, high power delivery, and long operational life in a single, safe device.

Applications

  • Professional power tools
  • Electric micromobility
  • Energy buffering for renewables and hydrogen systems
